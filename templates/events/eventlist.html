As we look back to our ancient times we see so much development in the world. From Big Bang to our daily activities, everything involves science. Its blessings are everything that fills our life with all comforts that were unimaginable even a few years ago. Moreover, Science has demonstrated that it is a supreme mechanism to explain the world, to solve problems and to fulfil human needs. A fundamental condition is its dynamic nature: the constant revision and re-evaluation of the existing knowledge. Every scientific theory is always under scrutiny and questioned whenever new evidence seems to challenge its validity. No other knowledge system has demonstrated this capacity, and even, the defenders of faith-based systems are common users of medical services and technological facilities that have emerged from scientific knowledge. In other words, Science is one of the most important channels of knowledge. It has a specific role, as well as a variety of functions for the benefit of our society: creating new knowledge, improving education, and increasing the quality of our lives. Accordingly, we must realize the favours that science has provided us and must always preach in using science in every excellent and efficient way possible. The Science Forum of Hindu College is a group of inspired students and devoted faculty members to facilitate open dialogue between the science departments of the college. Acknowledging the interdisciplinary nature of research, such a conversion becomes crucial. To inculcate a scientific temperament among students, and to effectively bring out science outside the classroom, we have planned and organized popular science events which have seen good participation in the past.The society conducts a variety of activities throughout the year.
